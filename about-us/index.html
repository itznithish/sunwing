About Us

Based in Bangalore, our company is a leading provider of high-quality solar water pumps, motors, controllers, solar panels, agricultural drones, and AeroFC flight controller boards. We are committed to offering the best performance at the most competitive prices, empowering farmers and businesses with reliable and energy-efficient solutions.we sell products designed with advanced engineering and built to withstand diverse Indian conditions, making them ideal for both agricultural and industrial use.

What We Do

With a strong focus on innovation, quality assurance, and trusted sourcing partnerships, we deliver top-notch products without relying on overseas or inconsistent supply chains. Our offerings—including the precision-engineered AeroFC flight controller boards—are carefully selected from reputed manufacturers to ensure reliability and performance. By partnering with established industry leaders, we maintain high standards, faster delivery timelines, and consistent product quality. Our agile supply approach allows us to remain cost-effective and responsive to evolving market demands. Backing our products is a robust pan-India installation and service network, ensuring seamless delivery, professional setup, and prompt after-sales support across urban and rural areas. Our dedicated team guarantees smooth operations and long-term satisfaction, making us a reliable partner for sustainable, technology-driven agricultural and energy solutions.
